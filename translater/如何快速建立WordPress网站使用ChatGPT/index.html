What Is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a chatbot developed by OpenAI that uses a language processing model to generate text based on user inputs. It has a wide range of applications, from generating content and translating texts to producing code.

пёҸGPTжҳҜз”ұOpenAIејҖеҸ‘зҡ„дёҖз§ҚиҜӯиЁҖеӨ„зҗҶжЁЎеһӢпјҢеҸҜд»Ҙж №жҚ®з”ЁжҲ·иҫ“е…Ҙз”ҹжҲҗж–Үжң¬гҖӮе®ғжңүе№ҝжіӣзҡ„еә”з”ЁпјҢеҢ…жӢ¬з”ҹжҲҗеҶ…е®№е’Ңзҝ»иҜ‘ж–Үжң¬пјҢд»ҘеҸҠз”ҹжҲҗд»Јз ҒзӯүгҖӮпёҸ

How Does ChatGPT Work

From a user standpoint, ChatGPT works in a very straightforward manner. You just need to input a question or prompt in the chatbox, and the AI tool will provide a relevant response.

д»Һз”ЁжҲ·зҡ„и§’еәҰжқҘиҜҙпјҢChatGPTе·ҘдҪңзҡ„ж–№ејҸеҫҲзӣҙи§ӮгҖӮдҪ еҸӘйңҖиҰҒеңЁиҒҠеӨ©жЎҶдёӯиҫ“е…Ҙй—®йўҳжҲ–жҸҗйҶ’пјҢз„¶еҗҺAIе·Ҙе…·дјҡжҸҗдҫӣзӣёеә”зҡ„еӣһзӯ”гҖӮ
But the process behind this advanced AI response is quite complex.

дҪҶжҳҜпјҢиҝҷдёӘй«ҳеәҰжҷәиғҪзҡ„еӣһзӯ”еӨ„зҗҶиҝҮзЁӢеҚҙеҫҲеӨҚжқӮгҖӮпёҸ

ChatGPT uses a machine learning technique called the Natural Language Processing (NLP) model. It enables computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language by combining aspects of linguistics and computer science.

дҪҝз”ЁжңәеҷЁеӯҰд№ жҠҖжңҜ called Natural Language ProcessingпјҲNLPпјүжЁЎеһӢгҖӮе®ғи®©и®Ўз®—жңәзҗҶи§ЈгҖҒи§ЈйҮҠе’Ңз”ҹжҲҗдәәзұ»иҜӯиЁҖпјҢйҖҡиҝҮз»“еҗҲиҜӯиЁҖеӯҰе’Ңи®Ўз®—жңәз§‘еӯҰзҡ„ж–№йқўгҖӮпёҸ

However, this is not a new model. In fact, most tools, like Google Translate and Siri, that offer features like word suggestion, plagiarism detection, and proofreading, also use the NLP model.

з„¶иҖҢпјҢиҝҷе№¶дёҚжҳҜдёҖз§Қж–°зҡ„жЁЎеһӢгҖӮе®һйҷ…дёҠпјҢеӨ§еӨҡж•°е·Ҙе…·пјҢеҰӮGoogleзҝ»иҜ‘е’ҢSiriпјҢжҸҗдҫӣзҡ„еҠҹиғҪпјҢеҰӮж–Үжң¬е»әи®®гҖҒ РҝР»Р°РіРёР°СӮжЈҖжөӢе’Ңж–Үжң¬дҝ®ж”№пјҢд№ҹдҪҝз”ЁдәҶNLPжЁЎеһӢгҖӮпёҸ

What makes OpenAI different is that it trains ChatGPT using the Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) method. It involves human feedback to measure and rank the responses based on their quality.

OpenAIдёҚеҗҢдәҺе…¶д»–жңәеҷЁеӯҰд№ жЁЎеһӢпјҢе®ғдҪҝз”Ёдәәзұ»еҸҚйҰҲж–№жі•пјҲReinforcement Learning from Human FeedbackпјҢRLHFпјүиҝӣиЎҢи®ӯз»ғгҖӮиҝҷз§Қж–№жі•еҢ…жӢ¬дәәзұ»FeedbackжқҘиҜ„дј°е’ҢжҺ’еәҸеӣһзӯ”зҡ„иҙЁйҮҸгҖӮпёҸ

[

](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2023/02/chatgpt-language-learning-diagram.webp)

In addition, engineers apply the Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) algorithm to finetune the reinforced learning procedure and produce more realistic responses from ChatGPT.

д»ҘдёҠж–Үеӯ—иҝҳжҳҜйҮҮз”ЁеҚҸи°ғpolicyдјҳеҢ–пјҲPPOпјүз®—жі•иҝӣиЎҢиҫ…еҠ©еӯҰд№ пјҢдҪҝChatGPTзҡ„еӣһзӯ”жӣҙеҠ зңҹе®һгҖӮпёҸ

According to OpenAI, ChatGPT can mimic a human-like conversational pattern. The dialogue format enables the chatbot to answer follow-up questions, admit its mistakes, challenge incorrect premises, and reject inappropriate requests.

ж №жҚ®OpenAIзҡ„жҠҘе‘ҠпјҢChatGPTеҸҜд»ҘжЁЎд»ҝдәәзұ»зҡ„еҜ№иҜқж јејҸгҖӮиҝҷз§Қж јејҸдҪҝchatbotиғҪеӨҹеӣһзӯ”и·ҹиҝӣй—®йўҳпјҢжүҝи®Өй”ҷиҜҜпјҢжҢ‘жҲҳдёҚжӯЈзЎ®зҡ„еүҚжҸҗе’ҢжӢ’з»қдёҚеҪ“зҡ„иҜ·жұӮгҖӮпёҸ

Those aspects make ChatGPT far more advanced than the existing AI-powered assistants like Siri or Alexa, as they are not trained to engage in back-and-forth conversations.
иҝҷж®өж–Үеӯ—еҸҜд»Ҙзҝ»иҜ‘жҲҗдёӯж–Үдёәпјҡ

йҖҷдәӣж–№йқўдҪҝChatGPTијғйҖІжӯҘж–јexistings AI-poweredеҠ©жүӢеҰӮSiriжҲ–AlexaпјҢеӣ зӮәе®ғеҖ‘дёҚиў«иЁ“з·ҙдҫҶйҖІиЎҢеӣһжҮүејҸе°Қи©ұгҖӮпёҸ

How to Set Up ChatGPT

Users need to create an account on OpenAIвҖҷs website before using ChatGPT. The process is quite straightforward вҖ“ all you have to do is provide some information, including your name, email address, and phone number.

з”ЁжҲ·йңҖиҰҒеңЁOpenAIе®ҳзҪ‘дёҠеҲӣе»әеёҗжҲ·жүҚиғҪдҪҝз”ЁChatGPTгҖӮиҝҷдёӘиҝҮзЁӢйқһеёёз®ҖеҚ•пјҢдҪ еҸӘйңҖжҸҗдҫӣдёҖдәӣдҝЎжҒҜпјҢеҢ…жӢ¬дҪ зҡ„еҗҚеӯ—гҖҒз”өеӯҗйӮ®д»¶ең°еқҖе’Ңз”өиҜқеҸ·з ҒгҖӮпёҸ

Without further ado, letвҖҷs take a look at the detailed step-by-step process of setting up a new OpenAI account.

"дёҚйңҖиҰҒиҝӣдёҖжӯҘзҡ„еҒҡеҮҶеӨҮпјҢи®©жҲ‘д»¬зӣҙжҺҘзңӢзңӢи®ҫзҪ®ж–°OpenAIеёҗжҲ·зҡ„иҜҰз»ҶжӯҘйӘӨиҝҮзЁӢгҖӮ"пёҸ

1. Navigate to OpenAIвҖҷs ChatGPT

Visit ChatGPTвҖҷs page and click Try ChatGPT to log in or create a new account. You can also get some information about this tool, including its training method, limitations, and examples of use cases.

и®ҝй—®ChatGPTзҡ„йЎөйқўпјҢе№¶еңЁе…¶дёҠй”®е…ҘвҖңе°қиҜ•ChatGPTвҖқд»Ҙзҷ»еҪ•жҲ–еҲӣе»әж–°иҙҰжҲ·гҖӮдҪ иҝҳеҸҜд»ҘиҺ·еҫ—иҝҷдёӘе·Ҙе…·зҡ„и®ӯз»ғж–№жі•гҖҒйҷҗеҲ¶е’ҢдҪҝз”ЁеңәжҷҜзҡ„дҝЎжҒҜгҖӮпёҸ
[

](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2023/02/chatgpt-page-on-openai-website-with-the-try-chatgpt-button-highlighted.webp)

2. Enter Your Email Address and Password

Create a new account by entering your email address and password, and click Continue.

[

](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2023/02/openai-create-new-account-page-with-the-email-and-password-field-highlighted.webp)

3. Confirm Your Email and Phone Number

After that, you will receive a verification request through email. Open it and click Verify email address.

[

](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2023/02/openai-account-verification-email-with-the-verify-email-address-highlighted.webp)

The button will direct you to OpenAIвҖҷs account onboarding page, where you have to input your name and phone number. Then, OpenAI will send a verification code to your phone number through WhatsApp or SMS.

4. Fill In Your Questions, Hit Submit, and Wait for a Response

Once youвҖҷre done with the onboarding process, you can start using the AI model by writing a question or prompt on the AI chat box. Hit enter and wait for the chatbot to respond to your query.

The speed of this response depends on how many people use the service at the moment.

[

](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2023/02/chatgpt-interface-with-the-chatbox-highlighted.webp)

Users can rate the response by clicking either thumbs up or down, helping the AI learn the best answer for the prompt.

It is also possible to try for a new response with the same prompt by clicking the Regenerate response button above the chat box.
